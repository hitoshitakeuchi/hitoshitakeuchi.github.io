Introduction

＜“Journey of Self-care”(2022)＞

Journey of Self-care, HD, 8min.39sec., 2022 Fence Line Text, Journey of Self-care Installation ver, 2022

In order to open up a perspective on self-care, I went to Okinawa and attempted to generate my own way of speaking by listening to the narratives of sculptors in Okinawa. Through the production of artworks, I will confirm whether or not his attempts are valid as a free indirect speech method.
