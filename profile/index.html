Profile

Media Artist (focus on Digital Image, Installation, VR/AR)

My primary interests are exploring the interface between real and virtual. I try to give reality to both real and virtual through my works. Born 1985 in Tokyo, Japan. Bachelor of Intermedia Art. Tokyo University of the Arts, Japan.
