Profile

Artist, Programmer, Researcher

Born 1985 in Tokyo, Japan. Bachelor of Intermedia Art, Tokyo University of the Arts. I’m mostly interested in human perception and media aesthetics theory, and I create video works, interactive installations and paintings. In the unique conditions of media technologies, I present a variety of works that critically redefines human sensory experience and existence.
