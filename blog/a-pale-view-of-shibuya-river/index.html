Introduction

＜“A Pale View of Shibuya River” (Installation, 2019)＞

It is a landscape of the past where Shibuya River flowed under Cat Street. Before the Tokyo Olympics 1964, the river around there is forced to flow underground using a conduit and no water flow can be seen on the ground.

Dates: September 20, 2019 - September 29

Location: BLOCK HOUSE
